About the webpage

Hello. Thanks for visiting this webpage. You must enable Javascript (if you have not already) in order to get the the functionality of this webpage.

This webpage is about getting recommendations for movies, books, authors, music, television shows, and games using TasteKid's API. This is a webpage I created using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and Font Awesome as a way of learning how to do front-end web develoment. This is a Thinkful project that I did as part of their front-end web development course.

My goal for this webpage was to give the user the ability to find recommendations and be able to favorite them. I spent about three weeks overall on this project because I wanted to see what I could do with it. Please take a look around. If you need help, just click the help button located on the other sections of the webpage.

What do you want a recommendation for?

This section is for searching for a recommendation. You can search for a recommendation by filling out the form below. By default, all types of recommendations are returned and the maximum number of recommendations retrieved is set to 20. Click the search button after you have finished filling out the form and are ready to see recommendations.

The recommendations for your search

This section is for viewing your recommendation search results after you have made a search. You can click on the tabs of a recommendation result for viewing different information about that particular recommendation. If you like a recommendation, you can add it to your favorites by clicking its favorite button. If you see that its favorite button has a black background with white text saying "Recommendation favorited", then that recommendation's name and type already exists in your favorite recommendations. You can navigate through the recommendation results by clicking on the directional arrows that has text "Next" or "Previous". Another way to navigate the recommendations is by entering a page number. By default, the maximum number of recommendations displayed per page is five.

Your favorite recommendations

This section is for viewing your favorite recommendations after you have favorited them. You can click on the tabs of a recommendation result for viewing different information about that particular recommendation. A recommendation can be deleted by clicking its delete button. You can navigate through the recommendation results by clicking on the directional arrows that has text "Next" or "Previous". Another way to navigate the recommendations is by entering a page number. You can change the way the recommendations are displayed by clicking on the button with the text "Settings" that exists below. By clicking that button you are able to filter and sort recommendations and change the maximum number of recommendations per page.

Your previous activities

This section is for viewing your activity history once you have made at least one search, added a favorite, or deleted a favorite. By default, all activities are shown, but you can choose which activity you want to view by selecting it. The only types of activities that can be shown are information about your searches, favorites, and deletes. The time in which each activity occurred is also presented. You can delete a listing by clicking the button with a trash can for that listing. This section may help you to understand your behaviour or just to review what you have done on this webpage.
